About The Doctor

Dr. Ajayakumar R

Dedicated to providing the highest quality medical care for infants, children, and adolescents.

Dr. Ajayakumar R is a distinguished Consultant in Paediatrics currently serving at the Taluk Headquarters Hospital in Alathur, Palakkad. With a deep commitment to child health, he brings extensive expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of pediatric conditions.

His approach combines evidence-based medicine with a compassionate, child-friendly environment, ensuring that both young patients and their parents feel comfortable and well-informed.

Our Expertise

Comprehensive Paediatric Services

Specialized healthcare services tailored to meet the unique needs of growing children.

Newborn Care

Comprehensive care for newborns, including routine checkups, feeding guidance, and monitoring early developmental milestones.

Vaccinations & Immunizations

Complete vaccination schedules to protect your child against preventable diseases from infancy through adolescence.

Growth & Development

Regular monitoring of physical, cognitive, and emotional development to ensure your child is reaching their full potential.

General Illness Treatment

Expert diagnosis and management of common childhood illnesses, infections, fevers, and respiratory conditions.

Nutritional Counseling

Guidance on healthy eating habits, managing fussy eaters, and ensuring adequate nutrition for growing bodies.

Allergy & Asthma Care

Management of childhood allergies and asthma, helping children lead active and healthy lives without restrictions.
